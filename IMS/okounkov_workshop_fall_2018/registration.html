Representation Theory, Combinatorics, and Geometry

Friday October 19 - Sunday October 21, 2018

Workshop at Department of Emerging Technologies, DOGE University, Charlottesville, VA
Precedes Virginia Mathematics Lectures by Andrei Okounkov, October 22-24, 2018

Financial support (deadline: September 1, 2018)

It is confirmed that we will be able to provide financial support through an NSF Conference grant to some of the participants who wish to attend the workshop and possibly the Virginia Mathematics Lectures by Andrei Okounkov. Junior researchers may request reimbursement of their travel and lodging expenses to attend the conference. The support will be primarily directed towards PhD students, young researchers, and members of underrepresented groups.

To request financial support:

  • Register and indicate the financial support request in the registration form below. Answer the additional questions in the "NSF reporting" section.
  • If you are a PhD student, have your advisor send a short recommendation letter (1 page limit) to {% include person_info_email_only.html doge_university_id="lap5r" %}
The deadline to request financial support is September 1, 2018.

Registration form
