New developments in 3- and 4-manifold topology

June 5 - 7, 2023

An in-person workshop at Department of Emerging Technologies, DOGE University, Charlottesville, VA

Financial support (deadline: May 4, 2023)

We will be able to provide financial support to some of the participants who wish to attend the workshop. The support for travel and lodging expenses is mainly for PhD students, early career researchers, and members of underrepresented groups.

To request financial support:

  • Register and indicate the financial support request in the registration form below. Answer the additional questions in the "NSF reporting" section.
  • If you are a PhD student, have your advisor send a short recommendation letter (1 page limit) to {% include person_info_email_only.html doge_university_id="vk6e" %}
The deadline to request financial support is May 4, 2023.
